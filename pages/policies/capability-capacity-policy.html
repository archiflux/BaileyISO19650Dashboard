Capability & Capacity Policy Statement

Bailey Partnership Group Limited is committed to effective management of our capability and capacity in accordance with BS EN ISO 19650 principles.

Policy Objective

To ensure that we have the right people, with the right skills, at the right time to deliver information management requirements on all projects.

2.0

Our Commitment

1. Proactive Capability Assessment

From the bid stage, we assess:

  • Required skills and experience
  • Software proficiency needs
  • Resource availability
  • Capability gaps

We identify gaps early and develop plans to address them through recruitment, training, or partnerships.

2. Strategic Capacity Planning

Regular resource meetings to:

  • Review current and forecast workloads
  • Allocate staff to projects effectively
  • Identify resource constraints
  • Balance demands across the organization
3. Competent Resource Allocation

All staff are assessed on their abilities, considering:

  • Qualifications and certifications
  • Relevant project experience
  • Software and technical training
  • Demonstrated competence

We match skills to project needs to ensure successful delivery.

4. Continuous Professional Development

Training program includes:

  • BIM induction for all new staff
  • ISO 19650 principles and best practices
  • Software and CDE training
  • Ongoing development in new techniques
5. Regular Review & Improvement

We continuously improve through:

  • Regular policy reviews
  • Feedback from staff and clients
  • Lessons learned from projects
  • Industry best practice adoption

Directors are ultimately responsible for ensuring this policy is implemented and maintained.

All staff are responsible for adhering to this policy and participating in capability and capacity planning activities.

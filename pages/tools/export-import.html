Data Storage Information

How Your Data is Stored

This dashboard uses browser local storage to save your data. This means:

  • Privacy: Your data never leaves your computer - it's stored locally in your browser
  • No account needed: No login or registration required
  • Browser-specific: Data is tied to the specific browser you're using (Chrome, Firefox, etc.)
  • Device-specific: Data won't sync between different devices or browsers
  • Persistent: Data remains even after closing the browser (unless you clear browser data)

Important: Regular exports are recommended to:

  • Backup your work
  • Transfer data to another device or browser
  • Share data with colleagues
  • Protect against browser data clearing
